## Getting Started

After creating your account, you'll be automatically guided through a personalized onboarding process. The system adapts to your needs and experience level.

### What to Expect

- **5-10 minutes** to complete
- **Personalized path** based on your goals
- **Progressive feature unlocking** as you explore
- **Skip optional steps** if you prefer

---

## Creator Assessment

The first step helps us understand your experience level.

### Questions You'll Answer

1. **Experience Level**: How familiar are you with content creation?
   - Beginner: Just starting out
   - Intermediate: Some experience
   - Advanced: Regular content creator
   - Expert: Professional creator

2. **Platforms**: Which platforms do you use?
   - Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, OnlyFans, etc.

3. **Content Types**: What do you create?
   - Videos, Images, Text posts, etc.

### Why This Matters

Your answers determine:
- **AI assistance level**: More or less guidance
- **UI complexity**: Simplified or advanced features
- **Help frequency**: How often you see tips
- **Feature recommendations**: What tools you see first

## FAQ

### Can I skip the onboarding?

Yes, but we recommend completing it to unlock all features and get personalized recommendations.

### How long does onboarding take?

Typically 5-10 minutes, depending on how many platforms you connect and goals you select.

### Can I change my answers later?

Yes! You can update your creator level, goals, and AI preferences anytime in your profile settings.

### What if I get stuck?

- Click the **Help** button for assistance
- Use the **Skip** button to move forward
- Contact support if you need help

### Do I need to connect all platforms?

No, connect only the platforms you use. You can add more platforms later from your dashboard.

### What happens after onboarding?

You'll be taken to your personalized dashboard with:
- Recommended next steps
- Quick actions for your goals
- Access to all unlocked features

### Can I retake the onboarding?

Yes, you can restart onboarding from your profile settings if you want to change your path.

### Are feature tours mandatory?

No, all tours are optional. You can dismiss them or take them later from the Help menu.

### How do I know what features are unlocked?

Check the **Features** page in your dashboard to see:
- ‚úÖ Unlocked features
- üîí Locked features with unlock requirements
- Progress toward unlocking new features
