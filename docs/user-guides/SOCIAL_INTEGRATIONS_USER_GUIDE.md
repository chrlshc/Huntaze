# Social Integrations User Guide

## Overview

This guide will help you connect and use TikTok and Instagram integrations to publish content directly from your dashboard.

## TikTok Integration

### How to Connect Your TikTok Account

1. **Navigate to Platforms**
   - Go to your dashboard
   - Click on "Platforms" in the sidebar
   - Select "Connect TikTok"

2. **Authorization Process**
   - Click "Connect TikTok Account"
   - You'll be redirected to TikTok's authorization page
   - Log in to your TikTok account if not already logged in
   - Review the permissions requested:
     - Access to your profile information
     - Ability to upload videos
     - Access to video analytics
   - Click "Authorize" to grant permissions

3. **Confirmation**
   - You'll be redirected back to your dashboard
   - Your TikTok account should now show as "Connected"
   - You'll see your TikTok username and profile picture

### How to Upload Videos to TikTok

1. **Access Upload Form**
   - Go to "Platforms" → "TikTok" → "Upload"
   - Or use the "Create Content" button and select TikTok

2. **Upload Your Video**
   - Click "Choose Video File" or drag and drop
   - Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI
   - Maximum file size: 500MB
   - Recommended duration: 15 seconds to 10 minutes

3. **Add Content Details**
   - **Caption**: Write your video description (up to 2200 characters)
   - **Privacy**: Choose who can see your video:
     - Public: Everyone can see
     - Friends: Only your friends
     - Private: Only you can see
   - **Comments**: Enable or disable comments
   - **Duet**: Allow or disallow duets
   - **Stitch**: Allow or disallow stitches

4. **Publish**
   - Review your content in the preview
   - Click "Publish to TikTok"
   - You'll see a progress indicator
   - Once complete, you'll get a confirmation with the video link

### TikTok Troubleshooting

**Connection Issues:**
- Make sure you're using a TikTok Business or Creator account
- Check that your account has posting permissions enabled
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account

**Upload Failures:**
- Verify your video meets TikTok's requirements
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure your TikTok account is in good standing

## Instagram Integration

### How to Connect Your Instagram Account

1. **Prerequisites**
   - You need a Facebook Page connected to your Instagram Business or Creator account
   - Your Instagram account must be a Business or Creator account (not personal)

2. **Connection Process**
   - Go to "Platforms" → "Connect Instagram"
   - Click "Connect Instagram Account"
   - Log in to your Facebook account
   - Select the Facebook Page connected to your Instagram
   - Grant the requested permissions:
     - Manage your Pages
     - Access Instagram Basic Display
     - Publish content to Instagram

3. **Verification**
   - Your Instagram account will appear as connected
   - You'll see your Instagram username and profile picture
   - The system will verify your account type

### How to Publish to Instagram

1. **Create New Post**
   - Go to "Platforms" → "Instagram" → "Publish"
   - Or use "Create Content" and select Instagram

2. **Upload Media**
   - **Images**: JPG, PNG (up to 8MB)
   - **Videos**: MP4, MOV (up to 100MB, 60 seconds max)
   - **Carousel**: Upload multiple images/videos

3. **Add Post Details**
   - **Caption**: Write your post description (up to 2200 characters)
   - **Location**: Add a location tag (optional)
   - **Alt Text**: Add accessibility descriptions for images

4. **Preview and Publish**
   - Review how your post will appear
   - Click "Publish to Instagram"
   - Monitor the publishing status
   - Get confirmation when published successfully

### Instagram Troubleshooting

**Account Connection Issues:**
- Ensure your Instagram is a Business or Creator account
- Verify your Facebook Page is properly connected to Instagram
- Check that you have admin access to the Facebook Page

**Publishing Failures:**
- Verify media meets Instagram's requirements
- Check that your caption doesn't contain prohibited content
- Ensure your account isn't restricted or shadowbanned

## General Troubleshooting

### Common Error Messages

**"Invalid Token"**
- Your authorization has expired
- Go to account settings and reconnect the platform

**"Upload Failed"**
- Check your internet connection
- Verify file format and size requirements
- Try uploading again after a few minutes

**"Account Not Eligible"**
- For TikTok: Switch to Business or Creator account
- For Instagram: Ensure Business/Creator account with connected Facebook Page

**"Rate Limited"**
- You've exceeded the platform's posting limits
- Wait before trying to post again
- Check platform-specific posting guidelines

### Getting Help

If you continue to experience issues:

1. **Check Platform Status**
   - Visit TikTok or Instagram's official status pages
   - Platform outages can affect connectivity

2. **Review Account Settings**
   - Ensure your accounts meet platform requirements
   - Check that permissions haven't been revoked

3. **Contact Support**
   - Use the in-app help feature
   - Provide specific error messages when reporting issues

## Best Practices

### Content Guidelines
- Follow each platform's community guidelines
- Ensure you have rights to all content you upload
- Use appropriate hashtags and descriptions
- Consider optimal posting times for your audience

### Account Management
- Regularly check connection status
- Monitor your content performance through platform analytics
- Keep your account information up to date

### Security
- Never share your login credentials
- Regularly review connected applications in your platform settings
- Disconnect unused integrations
