# Huntaze Signup Guide

Welcome to Huntaze! This guide will help you create your account and get started.

## How to Sign Up

### Option 1: Sign Up with Email (Magic Link)

1. **Visit the signup page** at [huntaze.com/signup](https://huntaze.com/signup)
2. **Enter your email address** in the signup form
3. **Click "Continue with Email"**
4. **Check your email** for a magic link from Huntaze
5. **Click the link** in the email to verify your account
6. **You're in!** You'll be redirected to complete your onboarding

**Benefits:**
- No password to remember
- Secure authentication
- Quick and easy

### Option 2: Sign Up with Google

1. **Visit the signup page** at [huntaze.com/signup](https://huntaze.com/signup)
2. **Click "Continue with Google"**
3. **Select your Google account** in the popup
4. **Grant permissions** when prompted
5. **You're in!** You'll be redirected to complete your onboarding

**Benefits:**
- One-click signup
- Use your existing Google account
- Automatic profile setup

### Option 3: Sign Up with Apple

1. **Visit the signup page** at [huntaze.com/signup](https://huntaze.com/signup)
2. **Click "Continue with Apple"**
3. **Authenticate with Face ID/Touch ID** or your Apple ID password
4. **Choose email visibility** (hide or share your email)
5. **You're in!** You'll be redirected to complete your onboarding

**Benefits:**
- Privacy-focused (can hide your email)
- Secure with Apple's authentication
- Quick signup process

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive the magic link email

**Check these common issues:**

1. **Check your spam folder** - Sometimes emails end up there
2. **Wait a few minutes** - Email delivery can take 1-5 minutes
3. **Check the email address** - Make sure you entered it correctly
4. **Try again** - Click "Try a different email" and re-enter your email

**Still not working?**
- Contact support at support@huntaze.com
- Include the email address you used

### The magic link expired

Magic links expire after **24 hours** for security.

**Solution:** Go back to the signup page and request a new magic link.

### Google/Apple sign-in isn't working

**Common issues:**

1. **Pop-up blocked** - Allow pop-ups for huntaze.com in your browser
2. **Third-party cookies disabled** - Enable cookies for authentication
3. **Browser extensions** - Try disabling ad blockers temporarily
4. **Incognito/Private mode** - Try in a regular browser window

**Still not working?**
- Try signing up with email instead
- Contact support at support@huntaze.com

### I'm getting a "CSRF token" error

This is a security error that can happen if:
- Your session expired (you left the page open too long)
- You have cookies disabled
- You're using an outdated browser

**Solution:**
1. **Refresh the page** and try again
2. **Enable cookies** in your browser settings
3. **Update your browser** to the latest version
4. **Clear your browser cache** and try again

### The page is loading slowly

**Tips for better performance:**

1. **Check your internet connection**
2. **Close other browser tabs** to free up memory
3. **Try a different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
4. **Disable browser extensions** temporarily

**Recommended browsers:**
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

## Privacy & Security

### What data do we collect during signup?

**Email signup:**
- Your email address
- Signup timestamp
- Device type (mobile/desktop)
- Browser type

**OAuth signup (Google/Apple):**
- Your email address
- Your name (from your Google/Apple account)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Signup timestamp
- Device type

### How do we protect your data?

- **Encrypted connections** - All data is transmitted over HTTPS
- **Secure authentication** - Magic links expire after 24 hours
- **CSRF protection** - Prevents unauthorized form submissions
- **GDPR compliant** - You can request your data or deletion anytime
- **No password storage** - Magic links mean no passwords to leak
